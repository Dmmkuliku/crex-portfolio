Overview of the 7th convocation of the eastern africa statistical
training centre(EASTC)
The 7th convocation of Eastern Statistical Training Centre of the year 2024 continuesthe tradition within Academic institutions of gathering together academicians, student, alumni, benefactors, and industry professionals as stake holders of the university community. It is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching,re-search, and proffetional development.

This year's convocation has selected the topic "The emerging data science and digital innovation ecosystem of Tanzania" as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year's 18 November Africa Statistics Day. The theme states "Strengthening data systems by modernizing production and use of agriculture Statistics: Informing policies with the view to improving resilience in agriculture,nutrition and food security in Africa"

African Statistics Day is celebreted each year. The commemoration aims among others,to increase public awareness about the important role chich statistics play in all aspects of social and economic development. This year's convocation aims to uncover the trends so far, government support, and emerging efforts in the public and private sectors that combine data use and technology in solutions that can be scaled to address challenges within Tanzamian's and African's agriculture and other sectors.

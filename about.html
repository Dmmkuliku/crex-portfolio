About Us

Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre serves eighteen Countries which are on
the Eastern and Southern side of Africa, namely Botswana, Ethiopia, Kenya,
Lesotho, Malawi, Seychelles, Somalia, Swaziland, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia,
Zimbabwe, South Africa, Mauritius, Namibia, Eritrea, Sudan, Gambia and South Sudan.
